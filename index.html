ARALDITE

A strong, solvent-free adhesive suitable for all materials, ideal for durable bonding and repairs. Perfect for complicated jobs which require adjustment thanks to a long working time. Araldite adhesive sets by the interaction of an epoxy resin with a hardener. ... It is available in many different types of pack, the most common containing two different tubes, one each for the resin and the hardener. Other variations include double syringe-type packages which automatically measure equal parts. For walls, ensure loose plasterwork and wallpaper has been removed. Always ensure surfaces are clean, free from dust and grease. Cut the nozzle of the tube before use. The adhesive will set in 20 seconds with pressure applied to the joint. Araldite Standard is strong and waterproof, making it the ideal bonding solution for load bearing or exterior use

Holdite

Holdtite is a single component liquid Sealant used for joining the metal pipes and sockets. It is used to make a leak proof joint in domestic plumbing as well as in industrial piping for transporting water, steam and variety of chemicals. It is non hardening shellac based compound. Good resistance to corrosion. Fast drying and spreads evenly Er fills gaps effectively. Good heat resistance (-20 to 120°C). Hydrostatic pressure – 20 Kg/cm2 (bar).Pipe joints can be dismantled when required. Pipe joints are 100% leakproof. Black in nature. Available in sizes from 50gm,100gm,200gm,500gm,1kg.
